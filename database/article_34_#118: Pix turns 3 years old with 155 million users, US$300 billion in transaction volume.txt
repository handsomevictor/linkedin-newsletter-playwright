‚ÄúBrazil has a trading market, a more financial public with a speculator profile. Argentina was like that a few years ago. ‚ÄúWe have a product that does not depend on market volatility. We saw that our product is a little ahead of what Brazil is today. We could trade, but that doesn't really fit our mission. It's a casino. But it is a matter of time for Brazil to also enter this type of adoption.‚Äù
